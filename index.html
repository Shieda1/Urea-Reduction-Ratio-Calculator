What is the urea reduction ratio calcualtor?
The urea reduction ratio calcualtor is a tool that checks if the performed dialysis was of good quality.
How to calculate urea reduction ratio?
To calculate urea reduction ratio you should use the following formula:
urea reduction ratio = (1 - (post dialysis urea level / pre dialysis urea level)) * 100
Why use urea reduction ratio calcualtor?
The urea reduction ratio calcualtor is used in order to check if the performed dialysis was of good quality.
